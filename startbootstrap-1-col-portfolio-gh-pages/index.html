On the Inadequcy of Language - An Essay

A short essay on the neverending, human desire for communication and its inevitable unfullfillment.

"As Sartre had philosophically noted and the sociologist Goffman had socially observed: we are always in a state of 'performance' (always presenting an image of the self, always 'existing'). And so, silence betrays us just the same - communicating a 'state of self' to others that we do not wish to be understood as. We are condemned to be free said Sartre, maybe we are equally condemned to communicate."
